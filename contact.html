Need Immediate Support?

If you or someone you know is in crisis, please reach out to these resources:

  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 988 (24/7, free and confidential)
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • Emergency Services: Call 911 if you're in immediate danger

You matter. Help is available. Please don't hesitate to reach out.
